## CORE PRODUCT IDEA

VidyaFrame is an educational resource platform where teachers and schools can:

1. Browse educational charts
2. Browse worksheets
3. Browse certificates
4. Browse posters
5. Apply school branding
6. Download PDF/PNG

The main focus is NOT AI generation.

The main focus is:

* Ready-made educational resources
* Fast download
* School branding
* Huge searchable library
